Reengineering radioisotope production


Nusano is a private company that intends to bring stability to the underserved market of radioisotopes. We have developed a high current ion source and linear accelerator that allows us to remove the current limitations of radioisotopes manufacturing for medical application and innovation. The facility will include full processing for cGMP radioisotope production.

By turning scarcity into abundence, Nusano is replacing what's plausible with what's possible.

Medical Radioisotopes

Radioisotopes are extraordinarily valuable in the medical field, enabling diagnostic imaging and therapeutics to detect illnesses and fight early-stage cancers. 

Declining Global Supply

The high operating cost of existing production methods, combined with a declining number of production facilities, is causing a global bottleneck that requires a new approach.

"From research into fundamental [Actinium] chemistry to the most promising clinical trials, the development of Actinium radiopharmaceuticals is slowed by the small supply and resulting high cost that makes it inaccessible to many researchers

Key Advantages

Unique Technology

Patented, Breakthrough particle beam technology is capable of generating heavy ions orders of magnitude greater than any existing comparable source.

Industrial Scale Production

To meet the declining supply of radioisotopes worldwide, the Nusano accelerator facility will produce key medical radioisotopes at scale.

Broad Range of Products

The facility can produce several otherwise unavailable or affordable radioisotopes, enabling the development of personalized cancer therapies

Eco-Friendly

Nusano's accelerator consumes less energy to produce the same amount of radioisotopes compared to our competitors

Uranium Free Production

Our production method does not require the use of Uranium in its production process

Low Footprint

The facility houses a linear accelerator that is 10% of the size of competing linear accelerators
